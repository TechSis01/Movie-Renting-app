German Sherperd

German shepherds are one of the most intelligent dog breeds. Because they are so smart, they love to learn and are easy to train.

  • Scientific name:Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Lifespan: 9 - 13years
  • Habitat:Land(Domestic animals, love to be around family)
  • Average weight:75 - 90 pounds

Generally considered dogkind's finest all-purpose worker, the German Shepherd Dog is a large, agile, muscular dog of noble character and high intelligence. Loyal, confident, courageous, and steady, the German Shepherd is truly a dog lover's delight
